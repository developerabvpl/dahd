## How the research was produced

Three deep-research passes, each fanning out 5–6 parallel web searches, fetching primary sources, and adversarially verifying every falsifiable claim 3-vote style. Only claims that survived verification are stated as fact in these docs; refuted and unverified claims are tracked in [05-open-questions.md](05-open-questions.md).
