### UPLDB lineage

- Created out of GoI sanction **No. 3-15/97-AHT dated 03 March 1998**
- Mandate: cover **all breedable bovines** under organised breeding, implement the State Breeding Policy
- Administers **Rashtriya Gokul Mission** (state component) and **NAIP** (Nationwide Artificial Insemination Programme — current cycle 01-May-2025 to 31-Mar-2026)
- Sex-sorted semen AI is offered at **₹100/dose** (reduced from ₹300 at statewide rollout)
- Government Orders published at animalhusb.upsdc.gov.in/en/government-order

### PCDF

- Formed in **1962** as the apex state-level dairy cooperative federation, headquartered in Lucknow
- Markets under the **Parag** brand
- **13 dairy plants across 19 milk unions** (three-tier cooperative structure)
- **Constituent milk unions publish tenders directly** on their own sites — e.g. Varanasi Milk Union at https://paragvns.com/tenders (PCDF CFF Ramnagar) — a separately addressable vendor channel beyond the central UP e-tender portal

## Flagship state schemes

### Nand Baba Milk Mission (media-branded "Operation-4")

Bundles four sub-schemes:

| # | Scheme | Subsidy structure |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Mukhyamantri Swadeshi Gau Samvardhan Yojana** | Up to ₹80,000 subsidy for 2-cow units of improved indigenous breeds (Sahiwal, Gir, Tharparkar, Gangatiri, Haryana) bought from outside UP |
| 2 | **Mukhyamantri Pragatisheel Pashupalak Protsahan Yojana** | ₹10,000 for 8–12 kg/day yield; ₹15,000 for >12 kg/day on indigenous breeds |
| 3 | **Nandini Krishak Samriddhi Yojana** | 25-cow Gir/Sahiwal/Tharparkar/Gangatiri units. Project cost ₹62.5 lakh, max subsidy ₹31.25 lakh under 15% beneficiary / 35% bank / 50% subsidy split, 3-phase disbursement |
| 4 | **Mini Nandini Krishak Samriddhi Yojana** | Smaller-unit variant |

> The 15/35/50 split is specific to Nandini and certain Kamdhenu variants. Do **not** generalise to NLM, vaccine procurement, or gaushala grants — those use different patterns.

### 1962 toll-free helpline & 520 Mobile Veterinary Units (MVUs)

- Launched **26 March 2023** by CM Yogi
- Programme value: **₹201 crore** under LH&DC
- Helpline operated by **GVK EMRI** on behalf of AHD
- **Ziqitza Healthcare (ZHL) operates 92 MVUs across 14 western districts** on a 50/50 fixed-route + on-demand model. Integrated Command & Control Centre structure across 5 zones.
- The remaining ~428 MVUs are operated by other vendors across other zones — confirm before pitching MVU integration

### UP State Bio-Energy Policy 2022 — cow-dung-to-wealth lane

Jointly led by AHD + Agriculture:

- **100% electricity duty waiver for 10 years** from commercial commencement
- Waiver of stamp duty on rent/lease/sale deeds
- Waiver of development authority development charges
- Opens an EPC vendor lane for biogas / compost plants at gaushalas
