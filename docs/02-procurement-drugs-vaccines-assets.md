# 02 — Procurement: Drugs, Vaccines & Assets

> The recurring demand map for vendors. Every figure below is verified or flagged.

## Three central scheme buckets drive almost all procurement

| Bucket | What's bought | Funding | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| **NADCP** (FMD + Brucellosis) | FMD vaccine, Brucella S19 vaccine, **cold-chain cabinets** (ice liners, refrigerators) | 100% central | FMD biannual (Sept–Oct, Mar–Apr, 45-day windows). Brucella: annual cohort — female calves 4–8 months, once-in-lifetime |
| **LH&DC / ASCAD** | HS, BQ, CSF, anti-rabies, Ranikhet/NCD, Gumboro, Fowl Pox vaccines + AI consumables + MVU outfitting | 60:40 centre:state (90:10 hill/NE, 100% UTs) | Annual rate contracts |
| **Pashu Aushadhi** | Generic veterinary medicines, vitamins, hormones, minerals | LHDCP 2024-26: ₹3,880 cr total; Pashu Aushadhi ~₹75 cr | Continuous. Routes mostly via PM-KSKs / cooperatives — slight nuance for direct state AHD procurement |

### NADCP key facts

- **Total outlay**: ₹13,343 cr over five years (2019-20 to 2023-24); continuation under LHDCP 2024-26 confirmed
- FMD vaccination: **biannual at six-monthly intervals**, targeting **100%** of cattle, buffalo, sheep, goat and pig populations
- **UP-specific**: ~520.36 lakh FMD doses **per round × 2 rounds/year**, two 45-day windows (Sept–Oct, Mar–Apr). Source: state-adjacent aggregator — order of magnitude consistent with national round figures of 16.91–24.84 cr doses
- Brucellosis: once-in-lifetime, Brucella abortus S19 strain, 100% female bovine calves aged 4–8 months — creates an **annually-renewing cohort** procurement need
- **Cold-chain procurement is bundled** with vaccines: "Procurement of cold cabinets (ice liners, refrigerators, etc.) and FMD vaccine" — state/UT implementing agencies execute the cold-chain side, centre executes the vaccine side. **Parallel recurring equipment lane**.
- **Animal registration runs on INAPH** (Information Network for Animal Productivity & Health), with ear-tagging mandatory
- Vaccinator remuneration floor: ₹3/dose, ₹2/ear-tag (Sept 2021 recommendation to raise FMD to ₹10 and Brucella to ₹12)

### ASCAD vaccine basket (under LH&DC)

Operative non-NADCP vaccines for UP:

- HS (Haemorrhagic Septicaemia)
- BQ (Black Quarter)
- FMD (state share above NADCP)
- Classical Swine Fever (CSF)
- Anti-Rabies
- Ranikhet / NCD (poultry)
- Infectious Bursal Disease (Gumboro)
- Fowl Pox

States are permitted to add PPR, anthrax and others.

## Rate-contract pattern (UP AHD direct)

UP AHD publishes its own rate contracts at https://animalhusb.upsdc.gov.in/en/approved-rate-list:

1. **"Veterinary Medicines / Vitamins / Hormones / Minerals"** rate contract
2. **"Rate Contract of Veterinary Equipments / Machineries & Instruments"** (parallel)

This rate-contract structure is the **dominant procurement model** for both drugs and assets — confirmed by 185+ veterinary medicine tenders tracked on aggregators, including FY 2024-25 vaccine rate contracts and ASCAD scheme rabies / blue-tongue tenders.

## Asset lane — 520-MVU programme (live)

- Launched **26 March 2023, ₹201 crore** under LH&DC
- 1962 toll-free helpline operated alongside
- **Ziqitza Healthcare (ZHL)** runs **92 MVUs across 14 western districts** on a 50/50 fixed-route + on-demand call-centre model
- Integrated Command & Control Centre across 5 zones, helpline based in Lucknow
- Vendor lanes:
  - Vehicle outfitting (chassis-up) per DAHD MVU specifications
  - Refurbishment / maintenance contracts
  - Telemedicine kits, portable diagnostic devices
  - **Dispensing / inventory mobile app for the on-board vet** ← HMIS-style angle
  - Manpower outsourcing, dashboards, fleet telematics

## Gaushala & feed/fodder asset lane

- **Kanha Gaushala** model: **₹165.89 lakh per site** for 500+ animal capacity — but administered by **UP Urban Development Department**, **NOT** Pashudhan Vibhag. Cross-department vendor targeting needed.
- **300+ gaushalas** targeted for biogas plant installation in the next phase, building on ~40-50 already piloted
- Backed by **UP State Bio-Energy Policy 2022** (10-yr electricity-duty waiver, stamp-duty waiver, dev-charge waiver)
- Asset basket: chaff cutters, milking machines, water troughs, fans, biogas plant assets, compost units, cattle crushes, chaff-cutter sheds, submersible pumps, boundary walls, veterinary clinics, fodder godowns

## Procurement mechanics

### Portals

- **UP GePNIC e-tender portal**: https://etender.up.nic.in/nicgep/app — primary channel for UPLDB, AHD, PCDF tenders. **UPLDB does NOT run a separate vendor portal**.
- **GeM (Government e-Marketplace)**: https://gem.gov.in — for catalogued drugs and equipment with product codes
- **NLM entrepreneur portal**: https://nlm.udyamimitra.in — for subsidy-backed loans under the National Livestock Mission

### Government Orders & Approved Rate List

- https://animalhusb.upsdc.gov.in/en/government-order
- https://animalhusb.upsdc.gov.in/en/approved-rate-list

### NLM three sub-mission framework

Entrepreneur opportunity organised into:

1. **Breed Development** — poultry, sheep, goat, piggery
2. **Feed & Fodder Development** — seed-chain strengthening, hay/silage/block units
3. **Innovation & Extension** — R&D, extension, livestock insurance

## Top 3 highest-frequency categories for vendor targeting

1. **FMD + Brucellosis vaccines and cold-chain cabinets** under NADCP — biggest, biannual, predictable
2. **ASCAD vaccines + veterinary medicines/hormones/vitamins** under UP AHD rate contracts — annual
3. **MVU outfitting + AI consumables + lab/diagnostic equipment** under LH&DC and the 520-MVU programme

## Empanelment checklist

### Portals to register on

1. **etender.up.nic.in/nicgep/app** (NIC GePNIC) — mandatory for everything
2. **gem.gov.in** — for catalogued drugs / equipment
3. **nlm.udyamimitra.in** — if pitching as entrepreneur/SaaS under NLM
4. **animalhusb.upsdc.gov.in** — monitor Approved Rate List + Government Orders weekly

### Documents typically required

- Drug Licence (Form 20B/21B for distributors; manufacturer licence if OEM)
- GMP / ISO 9001 / ISO 13485 (for devices)
- BIS / CDSCO certifications where applicable
- Manufacturer authorization (for distributors of branded vaccines)
- Past performance certificates (typically 3 years)
- GST, PAN, Udyam (MSME) registration
- EMD + Performance Bond — specifics vary per tender, confirm per RFP
