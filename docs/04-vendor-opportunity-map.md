## How to get empanelled — concrete checklist

### Step 1: Register on the portals (one-time)

- [ ] Create vendor account on **etender.up.nic.in** with DSC (Class 3, signing + encryption)
- [ ] Register on **GeM** as seller; classify as Drugs / Devices / SaaS as applicable
- [ ] Get **Udyam** registration for MSE preference (25% set-aside)

### Step 2: Documents pack (have ready)

- [ ] Drug Licence (Form 20B/21B distributor; manufacturer if OEM)
- [ ] GMP / ISO 9001 / ISO 13485 / WHO-GMP where applicable
- [ ] BIS / CDSCO certifications for vaccines and biologicals
- [ ] Manufacturer authorization letter (for distributors)
- [ ] Past performance certificates (last 3 years; ideally with state AHDs or central PSUs)
- [ ] GST + PAN + Udyam + EPF + ESIC
- [ ] Bank guarantee / EMD-ready liquidity (typical 2-5% EMD, 5-10% performance bond)

### Step 3: First moves (top 3 highest-frequency)

- [ ] Track and bid **FMD + Brucella vaccine rate contracts under NADCP** — biannual, predictable
- [ ] Track and bid **veterinary medicines/vitamins/hormones/minerals rate contract** — annual, anchor item
- [ ] Track and bid **MVU outfitting, AI consumables, lab/diagnostic equipment** — under LH&DC and the 520-MVU programme

### Step 4: HMIS-specific moves

- [ ] Watch animalhusb.upsdc.gov.in/en/government-order weekly for any **IT / ICT / pharmacy MIS** GO or EOI
- [ ] Pitch directly to ACS AHD office: "UPMSCL-equivalent supply chain for the veterinary side" with a 3-module phased proposal
- [ ] Approach ZHL as an integration partner for the MVU dispensing app
- [ ] List your SaaS on GeM under appropriate cloud-software category if eligible

## Three quick-win modules (lead the pitch with these)

2. **MVU mobile dispensing app** — anchored in the 520-MVU programme
3. **Central CMS warehouse mgmt with FEFO + quarterly PO cycle** — modelled on UPMSCL+DVDMS and RMSC e-Aushadhi
