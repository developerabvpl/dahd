## Open questions to chase

### Department & people

1. **Who below ACS Meshram actually signs** vaccine rate contracts vs equipment rate contracts? (Director Disease Control & Farms vs Director Admin & Development vs Director General?)
2. What is the documented **empanelment SOP**: required docs, site-inspection cadence, GMP/GLP audit norms?
3. What is the role of **DUVASU Mathura** in tender/empanelment? (Did not surface in research.)

### Numbers

4. UP's **20th Livestock Census** numbers — cattle, buffalo, sheep, goat, poultry totals — from the DAHD 20th Livestock Census PDF directly.
5. UP's confirmed **national milk-production rank and percentage share** — from BAHS 2023-24, not aggregator sites.
6. Year-by-year **budget outlays 2020-21 to 2026-27** for UP AHD / Dairy Development / Fisheries, with capex vs schemes vs salaries split.
7. **Veterinary hospital / dispensary / AI centre / gaushala counts** in UP (beyond the 520-MVU fleet).
8. Specific awarded values, awardees, L1 prices for **UP's FY24, FY25, FY26 FMD vaccine rounds, ASCAD procurements, AI consumables, MVU-outfitting** tenders.
9. Any **vendor blacklists** published by UP AHD?

### Software & digitisation

10. Does UP AHD operate any **central / divisional veterinary drug warehouse** infrastructure (with ILRs and deep freezers for vaccines)? Is there any published roster comparable to UPMSCL's warehouse list?
11. Has UP AHD issued any **RFP / EOI / MoU 2020-2026** for a veterinary HMIS / pharmacy MIS with NIC, CDAC, NDDB, or private vendors?
12. Is **GVK EMRI / ZHL's 520-MVU fleet** currently using any dispensing or inventory app that would need integration?
13. What is the post-2024 status of **LHDCP funding allocations to UP specifically** — NADCP continuation, ASCAD state share, Pashu Aushadhi rollout via PM-KSKs?

### Pain-point evidence base

14. Specific **CAG paras, PIB releases, news audits** documenting stock-outs, vaccine wastage, cold-chain breaks, expired-stock incidents on the UP veterinary side — needed to anchor the business case for the HMIS pitch. (Fetch failed on the CAG 2019 UP Drug Management report and the medicaldialogues "fake vet drugs" story.)

## How to chase these efficiently

- Direct visit / fax / email to **animalhusb.upsdc.gov.in** contact list — most authoritative
- File **RTI applications** for tender histories, budget outlays, empanelment SOP
- Use **etender.up.nic.in archived tenders** (search by AHD organization filter) for FY24-FY26 award values
- Pull **CAG state audit reports** for UP — Compliance and Performance audits 2020 onward
- Cross-reference **BAHS** (Basic Animal Husbandry Statistics) for census and production numbers
